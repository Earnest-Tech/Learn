Great premarital counseling matters: At Growing Self, we know that successful, happy marriages happen when two people work together to create a strong foundation built on communication, emotional connection, friendship, and agreement. The most satisfied and enduring couples are the ones who proactively invest in their marriage by learning the skills — and doing the work — that will keep their love strong.

No one teaches any of us, explicitly how to have a fantastic relationship. Our excellent premarital counseling and relationship class, “Lifetime of Love” fills the gap, and teaches you what you need to know to create a satisfying relationship that endures over the long haul. (Read “Why Premarital Counseling Can Make or Break Your Marriage“) We know that finding affordable premarital counseling can be difficult. That is why we set out to provide the type of counseling needed that is often overlooked due to budget constraints. Our online premarital counseling course will help you learn and understand tips on how to ensure the love between you and your significant other never fails.

Our six-hour Denver premarital counseling and relationship class (and our online premarital counseling class) teaches you specific strategies and vital relationship skills. You’ll leave each engaging class with strategic assignments, new ideas, and a plan to keep your partnership strong.

FYI: This class is open to all. While this Denver premarital counseling class and online premarital counseling class were originally designed for premarital couples, we’ve found over the years that SO many established couples benefit from it that we’ve opened it up to every couple who simply want to improve their relationship in an easy, direct, and inexpensive format.

Here’s What You’ll Learn…

The Secrets of Strong Couples

Set your relationship up for success by learning what small things healthy couples do every day to keep their love for each other strong, and avoid the common mistakes that can lead to hurt feelings, resentment, and disconnection.

Create Agreement With The Big Five

If you can communicate, you can work through anything. Learn positive communication strategies that will help you both feel heard, respected and understood. Find out what the most common communication mistakes are, and how to avoid them. Find out how to turn any conversation into an opportunity for deeper connection.

Hopes and Dreams

Learn the essential skill of how to manage disagreements constructively. As couples grow together, conflict inevitably occurs. Learn how to reduce the friction points in your relationship and solve problems together positively and productively.

Keep Romance Alive

Your love really can last a lifetime. Learn how to stay romantically connected to each other, keep intimacy alive, focus on pleasure and fun, and prioritize each other’s needs, so that you can keep the sizzle in your marriage for years to come.

Create Agreement With The Big Five

Money, Household Responsibilities, Religion, Kids and Sex are the “Big Five” problem areas for most couples. Learn how to get on the same page and create agreement in these important aspects of your marriage, to proactively prevent problems.

Hopes and Dreams

You’re not just getting married, you’re joining lives and creating a shared future. Learn how to accept and support each other as you both grow and evolve, and co-create the kind of meaningful, joyful life you want…. together.
