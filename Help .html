We specialize in helping our clients create Love, Happiness and success through positive, effective, evidence-based counseling and coaching.

We can help you grow as a person, create healthy, happy relationships, and achieve your most precious personal and professional goals. Our goal is to help you make positive changes in the most important parts of your life, and create the life, love, and career you want.

 What is "Evidence-Based" Counseling and Coaching?

Not all approaches to counseling and coaching are the same. Evidence-based approaches are those that have been found by research to be genuinely effective in helping you create real and lasting change. Unless therapy or coaching is grounded in these evidence-based approaches, it can feel wishy-washy and ineffective.

Learn about the different types of evidence-based counseling and coaching we practice here at Growing Self, so that you can make educated decisions about which approach is best for you.

 How do I get started?

We specialize in helping our clients create Love, Happiness and success through positive, effective, evidence-based counseling and coaching.

Creating real and lasting change in the most important parts of your life, like yourself, your relationship and your career is not an “event” but rather a growth process that begins when you enter into a relationship with an expert therapist or coach who can help you understand yourself, grow as a person, and learn new skills and strategies that enable you to create positive change. Because you’ll be working closely with your therapist or coach for a period of time it’s important that you feel good about who you are working with. If you would like to bypass the consultation session and jump right in, you can schedule a solution session instead.

 Do you take my insurance?

We specialize in helping our clients create Love, Happiness and Success. Our clients work with us to achieve personal growth, healthy relationships, self improvement and professional success through positive, effective counseling and coaching.

In contrast, health insurance covers “behavioral healthcare that is medically necessary treatment for a psychiatric condition.” Here at Growing Self, we do not specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of mental health conditions. Therefore, many of our core services are not considered “medically necessary treatments,” but rather what proactive people who are on a path of self-actualization and life satisfaction do to achieve their most precious goals. If that’s what you’re looking for, you are in the right place.

That said, under certain circumstances, we may be able to help you submit claims to your health insurance company if your work with us meets some specific criteria. Learn more about all of your affordable options, see the rates of the different providers on our team, and learn whether we can help you use your insurance benefits on our Rates and Insurance page.

 How often will I meet with my counselor or coach?

Great question! That depends on a few variables including your goals, your current circumstances, and where you are in your growth process.

Often, people start by scheduling weekly sessions. As they gain traction, they may move to every-other-week meetings (or longer) in order to have more time to practice the new ways of being that they’re learning in their sessions.

Your counselor or coach will give you their honest recommendations once they learn more about you and your unique situation.

 Do you offer counseling by text?

We do not. We provide real, meaningful, powerful, evidence-based counseling and coaching that’s designed to help you create real and lasting change in yourself, your life, and your relationships.

This involves having an authentic relationship with a therapist or coach who can see you and hear you so that they can truly know you, understand you, and help you. Meeting face-to-face online allows your coach to read your body language, or see the fleeting expression on your face after they ask a challenging question, or notice if you’re experiencing a big emotion. That’s why your work with us is transformation: We go deeper.

If we work together, you’ll be having a real relationship with a real coach or therapist who is invested in you.

We don’t do “cookie cutter” because you’re not a cookie. You won’t get cliché advice or canned responses from us. Instead, you’ll get the full attention of a highly trained, smart, caring expert who knows YOU.

You’re an individual with a one-of-a-kind life. We offer you a true growth experience, and that means we both need to show up in an authentic way.

We’re ready to do that, with you.
